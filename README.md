#Project 1: Watch & Sip

## Project Description: 

```
Our goal is to solve boredom during this quarantine. The user will visit the site and, upon answering a few questions,
will be presented with the perfect movie and its drink.
```

## User Story:

```
AS A quarantined member of society
I WANT to find a movie and drink that fits my needs
SO THAT I can occupy my time
```

## Acceptance Criteria:
```
WHEN I enter the site
THEN I am presented with an option to choose a top movie or customize
WHEN I click customize
THEN I am presented with a series of questions
WHEN I answer these questions
THEN I am presented with a movie to watch and a drink pairing
WHEN I am presented with a movie
THEN I am presented with its title, thumbnail, rating, and trailer
WHEN I am presented with a drink
THEN I can see its ingredients and recipe
```
